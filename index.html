Community Curriculum Index Page

This page is a short-term solution to provide index for Participation curriculum in it's various states of development and testing.

Curriculum Attributes

learning area

What specific team, or area of Mozilla this curriculum was developed for and with.

Audience

The curriculum audience (who we had in mind when we created it)

workflow state (draft, testing, published)

Where we feel this is in the curriculum development workflow

learning outcome

What skills, knowledge and attitudes this curriculum is intended for.

Participation Leadership Framework

If this curriculum fits into our framework, where.
